America Dream Nachos

Ingredients
  • torilla chips
  • Mex blend cheese
  • Oxaca cheese (sliced)
  • Cooked hamburger
  • Jalepenos, black olives, banna peppers
  • Diced tomoto
  • Shredded lettuce (sub arugala or kale)
  • shredded chicken
  1. Lay chips in an oven safe cooking pan
  2. Layer mex blend cheese, hamburger, and jalepno
  3. Add another layer of chips and layer chicken, tomoato, black olives and banana peppers
  4. Add oxaca cheese
  5. cook until top layer of cheese starts to brown
  6. add lettuce, and sour cream or hot sauce depending on palette
