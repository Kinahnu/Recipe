The Perfect Steak

The human race has been enjoying steak for thousands of years, but still some find it a hard untaking. Well lucky for you i have the perfect barebones recipe so that you'll never have to worry about it again.

Ingredients

  • So the only ingredients you'll need are a fresh slab of steak, salt, and pepper
  1. So to begin you're going to want to oil a pan and leave it on medium high until it warms up
  2. Next you'll want to grab the steak and season then grab with tongs and sear all of the edges
  3. Once you've done that its time to set a 5 minute timer and lay it on its side
  4. once the timer goes off re-set it for 3 minutes and allow the other side to cook and then you're done!
